After approximately three months participating in the internship program at **AWS**, I had the opportunity to work in a professional environment, learn a wide range of cloud technologies, and contribute to the development of a real-world project. This internship was a valuable experience that helped me grow both technically and professionally. Below are my personal reflections and feedback on the internship program.

## Overall Evaluation

### 1. Working Environment

The working environment at AWS is highly professional while remaining open and welcoming. Throughout the internship, I always received support from my mentor and teammates whenever I encountered difficulties. In addition, the company encouraged interns to actively explore and experiment with new technologies instead of simply performing repetitive tasks, which greatly enhanced my learning experience.

### 2. Mentor Support

My mentor closely guided my learning and development throughout the internship. Whenever I encountered technical challenges, I received valuable suggestions and guidance rather than direct solutions. This approach encouraged me to develop independent learning skills, analyze problems systematically, and find appropriate solutions before seeking additional assistance.

### 3. Relevance to My Academic Major

The tasks assigned to me were closely related to my Information Technology major. By developing a web game project on AWS, I was able to apply my knowledge of programming, Web API development, database systems, computer networking, and cloud computing in a practical software project.

### 4. Learning and Development Opportunities

This was the most valuable aspect of my internship experience. I had the opportunity to work with various AWS services, including **Amazon EC2**, **Amazon VPC**, **Amazon S3**, **Amazon Cognito**, **Amazon DynamoDB**, **AWS Lambda**, **Amazon API Gateway**, **Amazon SQS**, **Amazon CloudWatch**, **AWS X-Ray**, and **AWS Amplify**. In addition to completing the hands-on labs in the AWS Workforce Bootcamp, I applied these services directly to my project, allowing me to gain a deeper understanding of how to design, develop, and deploy a complete cloud-based system.

### 5. Working Culture

I highly appreciate the collaborative and knowledge-sharing culture within the team. Every member was willing to provide support whenever needed while encouraging interns to be proactive and contribute their own ideas. This created a positive working environment and helped me become more confident throughout my internship.

### 6. Internship Program

The internship program followed a well-structured learning roadmap that combined theoretical knowledge, hands-on laboratory exercises, and real-world project development. This learning approach enabled me to gradually understand AWS services before applying them in practice, giving me a comprehensive understanding of how cloud services work together within a complete system.

---

## Additional Questions

### What did I enjoy the most during my internship?

The most satisfying part of my internship was having the opportunity to develop a real-world project rather than focusing solely on theoretical learning. Throughout the development process, I encountered many technical challenges and had to research documentation, experiment with different approaches, and identify suitable solutions independently. This experience significantly improved my practical skills and increased my confidence in working with cloud technologies.

### What improvements would I suggest for the internship program?

In my opinion, the program could include more in-depth technical sharing sessions on system architecture, real-world deployment experiences, and discussion sessions between mentors and interns after each learning module. These activities would help interns better understand how theoretical concepts are applied in real business scenarios.

### Would I recommend this internship program to my friends?

**Absolutely.**

I would highly recommend this internship program to students majoring in Information Technology or related fields. It provides an excellent opportunity to learn AWS cloud technologies, gain hands-on experience with real AWS services, and develop a complete project under the guidance of experienced mentors. These experiences are extremely valuable for students preparing to enter the professional software industry.

---

## Suggestions and Expectations

- Continue maintaining the learning model that combines theory, hands-on practice, and real-world project development.
- Organize more technical sharing sessions focusing on system architecture, DevOps, and practical software engineering experiences.
- Increase opportunities for discussions between mentors and interns to address technical challenges and provide timely guidance throughout the internship.
- Provide additional workshops or seminars covering newly released AWS services and emerging cloud technologies.

I sincerely appreciate all the mentors and team members who continuously supported and guided me throughout the internship. This experience has provided me with valuable knowledge, practical skills, and a clearer career direction for the future. I hope to have the opportunity to continue contributing to AWS through future programs or community projects.
